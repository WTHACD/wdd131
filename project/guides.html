Step-by-Step Guides

Opening an Account with IC Markets

Follow these steps to create a trading account and start copy trading on cTrader:

  1. Go to the IC Markets website and click "Create Account."
  2. Fill in your personal details such as name, email, and date of birth.
  3. Provide proof of identity and address as required.
  4. Fund your account using one of the available payment methods.
  5. Download the cTrader platform and log in with your IC Markets account details.
  6. Start exploring and copying successful traders!

Best Practices for Risk Management

Minimize your risk while maximizing your copy trading potential with these strategies:

  • Diversify Your Investments: Do not put all your eggs in one basket.
  • Set Stop-Loss Orders: Protect your capital by setting stop-loss levels.
  • Monitor Your Positions Regularly: Keep an eye on your trades and adjust as necessary.
